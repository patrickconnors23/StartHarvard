About

Start@Harvard is a program run within the Cronin Center for Enterprise and Harvard Student Agencies that provides students with the technical skills and know-how to join an early-stage startup or work on their own ideas.

It will run as course offerings throughout the semester.

F.A.Q.

What's the cost?

> All classes are free for Harvard undergrads.

Are there additional benefits for students?

> Course benefits include internship opportunities, portfolio enhancement, and social events.

What's the time commitment?

> Students must attend class once a week for 1.5 hours. There will be optional homework during the week.

How is this different from CS classes offered by Harvard?

> Start@Harvard focuses on building things instead of studying them.

Who teaches the classes?

> Classes are taught by students who have TF'd a CS class and shown proficiency in their trade.
